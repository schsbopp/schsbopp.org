Welcome!!!

Band of Pirates Parents is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization whose mission is to support the St. Charles High School bands and affiliated activities, including Concert Band, Jazz Band, Marching Band, Color Guard, Winter Guard, and Drum Line. Our organization strives to develop community awareness and provide financial support for everyone participating in all aspects of the SCHS band programs.
